It all began in 2016 when i felt a ghoulish wave wash over me and grasp my guts with a desire to start programming. It was at this moment i realized i had a programming ghoul hatchling of my very own growing inside of me. Not wanting my hatchling to become a cantankerous little wretch i decided to feed it the right nutrients but this took a good deal of digging. I really began learning python on coursera and when i found that i could really see the utility i stepped back from coding for approximately 2mnths. At this point i found this article on Tech in Asia about becoming a developer.I started off with the courses listed there and after completing the frist two months found myself having the skill to branch out from this route and also the disire to pull from other resources.
